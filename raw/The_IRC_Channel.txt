There is also a bot available at the channel, nicknamed <i>bertha</i>. You can ask her by using an exclamation point followed by a keyword or a command. Like:
<pre class="codeblock">!py2exe</pre>
She also offers some useful commands to display an API or Manual page:
<pre class="codeblock">!apiref NodePath</pre>
<pre class="codeblock">!manual The IRC Channel</pre>
<pre class="codeblock">!source panda/src/grutil/heightfieldTesselator.cxx</pre>
