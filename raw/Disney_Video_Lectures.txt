<p>David Rose, from Walt Disney VR Studio, occasionally holds classes based on the fundamentals and working of Panda3D. Below are a few of these lectures.

<ul>
	<li>[http://video.google.com/videoplay?docid=-3062788944994284554&hl=en Characters Part I] Recorded October 8, 2003. Eggs, Characters and Actors, but audio quality is very poor.
	<li>[http://video.google.com/videoplay?docid=-1586200015760444650&hl=en Characters Part II] Recorded October 22, 2003. This is a recap of character animation in Panda3D.
	<li>[http://video.google.com/videoplay?docid=-7958937369732071881&hl=en PStats] Recorded February 24, 2004. Demonstrating the use of Panda's built-in performance analysis tool.
	<li>[http://video.google.com/videoplay?docid=5009786393476731454&hl=en Threading] Recorded March 26, 2008. The current state-of-the-art in multiprogramming in Panda.
	<li>[http://video.google.com/videoplay?docid=-3032355519509216354&hl=en Windows, Buffers, and Cameras] Recorded April 9, 2008. Fundamentals of managing windows and offscreen buffers, and connecting them up to cameras for rendering.
	<li>[http://video.google.com/videoplay?docid=7093299406745614529&hl=en Scene Graph] Recorded April 2, 2008. Fundamentals of Panda's scene graph, tricks you can do with it, and how it is implemented.
	<li>[http://video.google.com/videoplay?docid=59098437095671394&hl=en DistributedObjects] Recorded April 16, 2008. How to use Panda's DistributedObject system, for the Python developer.
	<li>[http://video.google.com/videoplay?docid=-4547040139284563409&hl=en DistributedObjects and the OTP Server] Recorded April 23, 2008. Panda's DistributedObject system, and how it relates to the VR Studio's OTP Server.
	<li>[http://video.google.com/videoplay?docid=-6551816888913680536&hl=en The OTP Server] Recorded April 30, 2008. What's inside the VR Studio's OTP Server (not part of Panda).
	<li>[http://video.google.com/videoplay?docid=2633778230802753163&hl=en The PRC System] Recorded May 7, 2008. All about Panda's run-time configuration system.
	<li>[http://video.google.com/videoplay?docid=8524155402201040926&hl=en Collisions] Recorded May 14, 2008. How to use Panda's built-in collision system, and how it works.
	<li>[http://video.google.com/videoplay?docid=922496949308495232&hl=en egg-palettize] Recorded May 21, 2008. How to use egg-palettize, and why you'd want to.
	<li>[http://video.google.com/videoplay?docid=8716922590223131644&hl=en interrogate] Recorded June 4, 2008. How interrogate works to generate Panda's Python/C++ interface.
	<li>[http://video.google.com/videoplay?docid=6429726243770234547&hl=en ODE in Panda] Recorded June 18, 2008. How Panda's built-in ODE integration is intended to be used.
	<li>[http://video.google.com/videoplay?docid=53318334729655998&hl=en Inside an Actor] Recorded July 2, 2008. How the Actor class is implemented, and how to use it.
</ul>
